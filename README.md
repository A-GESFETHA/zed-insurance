# ZED (Zero Earning Days)

AI-powered parametric income protection for India's gig workers.

## The Problem
India's 7.7 million gig workers lose 20-30% of monthly earnings during monsoons, extreme heat, and local shutdowns. Existing insurance covers health and assets, but NOT income loss.

## The Solution
ZED provides automatic income replacement when external disruptions stop work.

**How it works:**
- Workers pay ₹0.50-₹1.00 per delivery
- We monitor weather, AQI, and platform data in real-time
- When disruption occurs, payout is automatic

**What we cover:**
- Heavy rain (>40mm in 3hrs) - ₹100 per hour
- Extreme heat (>42°C) - ₹150 per day
- Severe AQI (>300) - ₹100 per day
- Dark store closure - 50% of daily earnings
- Local curfew - Full day coverage

## Key Innovations
- **Dark store model:** Insure the store, not just the worker
- **Per-delivery premium:** Pay only when you earn
- **Multi-layer fraud detection:** Sensor + network + timing
- **Government data sources:** IMD, CPCB for legal defensibility

## Tech Stack (Planned)
React Native, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Python, OpenWeatherMap API, Razorpay
